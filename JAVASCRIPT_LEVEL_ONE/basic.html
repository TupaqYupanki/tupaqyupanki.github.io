Arithmetic operators:

JS have basic math functions like addition - "+", subtraction - "-", multiplication - "*", division - "/", exponation - "**" and remainder - "%"

The last one gives the reminder after division.

For example:
> 15 % 14
< 1
> 6 % 2
< 0
> 6 % 4
< 2
> 17 % 3
< 2

Other operators of JS you can find at the page https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Operators
